About Prelegal

Prelegal was founded on a simple belief: legal problems are easier and cheaper to solve before they escalate. Too many people wait until they're already in a dispute to seek legal guidance.

We partner with clients at the earliest stages — whether you're signing a contract, starting a business, or facing a potential dispute — and give you the tools and advice to act with confidence.
